MEGATECH – Leading the Way in Smart Lighting Solutions

WHO WE ARE

Established in 2013 in Mumbai by Mr. Ruchik Soni, MEGATECH began with a clear vision: revolutionizing the LED lighting industry. With a strong academic background in Electronics Engineering, Mr. Soni gained early hands-on experience while interning at an LED lighting manufacturing company. This pivotal opportunity inspired the creation of MEGATECH, which has since grown into a respected multinational brand in the lighting and solar energy sector.

MEGATECH specializes in a wide selection of LED lights and solar panels, engineered to deliver efficient, long-lasting performance across various environments. Our products are ideal for residential homes, commercial buildings, industrial facilities, highways, and both indoor and outdoor applications.
