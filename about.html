MEGATECH – Leading the Way in Smart Lighting Solutions

Established in 2013 in Mumbai by Mr. Ruchik Soni, MEGATECH began with a clear vision: revolutionizing the LED lighting industry. With a strong academic background in Electronics Engineering, Mr. Soni gained early hands-on experience while interning at an LED lighting manufacturing company. This pivotal opportunity inspired the creation of MEGATECH, which has since grown into a respected multinational brand in the lighting and solar energy sector.

MEGATECH specializes in a wide selection of LED lights and solar panels, engineered to deliver efficient, long-lasting performance across various environments. Our products are ideal for residential homes, commercial buildings, industrial facilities, highways, and both indoor and outdoor applications.

Trusted by global brands, MEGATECH has played a key role in lighting up architectural landmarks, interior spaces, warehouses, and large-scale infrastructure projects—driven by innovation, quality, and sustainability...... Read More..

At MEGATECH, our mission has always been clear: to deliver high-quality LED lighting solutions at competitive prices. Guided by our core philosophy—“Total Quality Assurance with Complete Customer Satisfaction”—we are committed to offering products that are not only reliable and efficient but also built to last.

We understand the importance of dependable lighting—solutions that combine performance, durability, and cost-effectiveness for long-term value. That’s why every MEGATECH product is thoughtfully designed and crafted using premium components and advanced engineering.

Our robust internal systems enable continuous improvement across every aspect of our business—from sales and procurement to manufacturing, customer support, and product innovation. This allows us to consistently excel in QCDS: Quality, Cost, Delivery Time, and Service.

Over the years, we’ve had the privilege of working with some of the most respected names in the industry. Our client portfolio includes renowned real estate developers like Rizvi, Rustomjee, and Hiranandani, as well as corporate giants such as Mphasis, Jindal Saw Ltd., and Maersk. We are especially proud to have contributed to a project with the Reserve Bank of India.

In 2019, we expanded into international markets by launching our export division—supplying LED lighting to Maersk Tankers in Dubai, Singapore, and the Netherlands. Since 2020, we’ve also been exporting lighting products to supermarket chains across Africa.

Our Vision

At MEGATECH, we are committed to creating a meaningful balance between technology, humanity, and the environment. As a forward-thinking company, we aim to be a positive force in driving sustainable global progress.

Our focus is consistently delivering excellence across every aspect of our business—Product, Quality, Cost, Service, and Relationships. Sustainability is at the core of our values, and we actively lead initiatives that contribute to a greener, more responsible future. Driven by a dedicated team of engineers and researchers, we continuously innovate to set new benchmarks in performance, efficiency, and environmental responsibility.
